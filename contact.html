Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 1-2 business days within Accra. For other regions, delivery may take 3-5 business days.

What is your return policy?

We do not offer return policy! Personalized items cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via SMS. You can use this to track your package on the delivery app (UBER, YANGO, BOLT).

Do you offer wholesale prices?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please contact us directly via WhatsApp or phone call to discuss wholesale options.
